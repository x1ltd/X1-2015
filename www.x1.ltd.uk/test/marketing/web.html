Web-2-Promotion

If you market through channels such as distributors, franchises and sales offices etc. you face the challenges related to centralised and decentralised marketing.

Centralised Marketing

If you choose centralised marketing, you have control and can deliver a consistent brand message. However, you run the risk of missing the opportunity to allow your local sales and marketing resources, who are closest to the customer, help you improve your marketing relevance for each location.

Decentralised Marketing

If you choose decentralised marketing, you put your marketing and branding in the hands of thousands of different shops and channels. Your key messages can become fuzzy and weak and the quality of your marketing materials can become inconsistent and shoddy.

Our Web-2-Promotion online portal can be branded to your company, allowing your authorised sales and marketing channels and employees to quickly and easily order what they need, when they need it, from wherever they are, via computer, smartphone or tablet.

Web-2-Promotion allows your channels and employees to fine-tune their marketing materials within your pre-defined parameters. You control the marketing strategy via the portal, and decide which marketing materials and merchandise such as promotional items are the most relevant to have for each location based on your strategy.

Web-2-Promotion will help you market more effectively across different territories and provide you with the means to track your channels and activities for further analysis, assessment and support.
