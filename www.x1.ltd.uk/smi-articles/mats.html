Re-Mats



How would you like to be on your customers' desks every single day of the year?

Marketing today is about being entertaining, helpful and informative to customers. Our inspirational mats and coasters can display different messages/inspirational quotes with replaceable digitally printed inserts.

It means that you could send your customers a mat or coaster with an inspirational quote or relevant message/scenery, then follow up with printed inserts to replace the old ones.

The mats come in three different sizes (coffee/glass small sizes, mouse mat and A3) and are perfect for B2B strategies, entertaining and informing your customers while keeping your contact details close to them.
