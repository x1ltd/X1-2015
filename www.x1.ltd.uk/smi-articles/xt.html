Chocolate Sprouts & X-Jars



With Christmas around the corner how do you thank your customers in a sweet and original way? How about sprouts (chocolate truffle, naturally!) – or an attractive glass jar filled with foil-wrapped chocolates?

Making customers feel special and appreciated will strengthen your brand and keep you at the forefront of their minds.

They can be delivered to you in bulk to distribute personally or we can mail them with a personalised message (there is no minimum order quantity).
