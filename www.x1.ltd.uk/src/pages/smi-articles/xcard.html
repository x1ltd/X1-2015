X-Card



X-Card is made of a polycarbonate material that can be printed using digital imaging technology. You can improve relevance, easy identification and tracking by personalising each card, including numbering and barcoding. Personal URLs can be added, allowing customers to access private information online.

X-Card is durable and waterproof, perfect for membership, loyalty and ID cards as well as applications where paper-based cards don’t last very long! And you can order small quantities as you need them. Minimum order is just 300 cards.
