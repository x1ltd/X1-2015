Print On Demand (POD)

These presentation and promotional products can be repurposed by replacing the digitally printed inserts, which can be personalised for effective and targeted communication. POD products work well at events, trainings, exhibitions, offices and point of sale (POS).


Movie presentations in a personalised folder


The movie folder is the right strategy to capture the attention and impress some of your best customers and prospects.

The folder is simple to set up:

- Plug the movie device into your PC

- Upload your movie files

- Print out your cover graphic on supplied inserts

- Slide into the presentation folder
