If you don’t make it easy for your customers to buy, you are promoting your competition.

Making it easy for your target to buy

After a successful engagement, we can help you make it easier for your customer to buy from you, rather than going somewhere else. We’ll pay special attention to your online presence, where your competitors are only one click away.

Besides incentives to help your customer purchase from you, we’ll make sure your buying process is clear, quick and frustration-free.

There is a wide choice of technologies that can help you make it easier for customers to buy, including

– QR codes
– IAM (Interactive Advertising Mailing)
– PURLs (Personalised URLs).

Not long ago, customers had to travel to the nearest store to buy something that they needed or wanted. Often consumers see a product they like online, but don’t have the time to got out and get it.

If you do not make it easy for your customers to buy you are promoting your competition help your customers buy your products/services in minutes without having to drive to a physical store. Our technologies will make it easier for them to go online and buy.

Digital Channels (email, digital print, mobile, web).

We can use geo-mapped direct mail to make sure your customers receive the address and map for the store that is closest to where they live — making it easier for them to buy. We can output personal numbers on your promotional pieces that will encourage recipients to go either to your nearest shop or online to see if they won a prize. Driving more customers to your store in this way increases the chances of sales productivity and allows data collection opportunities.

We can also output different coupons and/or incentives that link to different profiles in order to increase the relevance of your offer and drive more consumers to your store. Relevant incentives make it easier for a consumer to buy from you.
